# The Three Pillars of DIY Investing Explained

## Project Overview

**Purpose:** This video introduces the foundational Three Pillars framework that DIY-Investors.com is built on. It's educational content that positions our methodology and helps viewers understand how to approach stock analysis systematically.

**Target Viewer:** DIY investors who feel overwhelmed by all the investing advice out there. They want a clear framework to organize their research and make confident decisions.

**Key Message:** Successful investing requires balancing three perspectives: fundamental analysis (company quality), technical analysis (timing), and news-flow (context). Each pillar matters, and they work best together.

**Call to Action:** Join Inner Circle to access weekly stock analysis using the Three Pillars framework.

---

## Content Outline

### Hook (0:00-0:10)
- "Everyone tells you to research stocks, but nobody tells you WHAT to research."
- Promise: By the end, you'll have a clear framework for analyzing any stock.

### Main Content (0:10-8:00)
1. **Section 1: Pillar 1 - Fundamental Analysis (0:30-3:00)**
   - What it is: Company quality, financials, business model
   - Why it matters: Knowing WHAT you own
   - Example: Strong fundamentals vs weak fundamentals
   - B-roll: Balance sheet, P/E ratios, earnings growth

2. **Section 2: Pillar 2 - Technical Analysis (3:00-5:30)**
   - What it is: Price action, trends, support/resistance
   - Why it matters: WHEN to buy/sell
   - Example: Great company, wrong time vs right entry point
   - B-roll: Stock charts, moving averages, support levels

3. **Section 3: Pillar 3 - News-Flow (5:30-7:30)**
   - What it is: Market context, sentiment, catalysts
   - Why it matters: WHY the market moves
   - Example: Earnings surprise, sector rotation
   - B-roll: News headlines, earnings reports, sector performance

### Conclusion (7:30-9:00)
- Recap: Fundamentals = WHAT, Technicals = WHEN, News-Flow = WHY
- All three work together - don't ignore any pillar
- Call to action: Inner Circle members get weekly analysis using all three pillars
- Next video tease: Deep dive into Fundamental Analysis
