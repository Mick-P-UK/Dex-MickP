## Pillar 2: Technical Analysis

### Key Concepts to Cover
- Trend identification (uptrend, downtrend, sideways)
- Support and resistance levels
- Entry and exit timing
- Volume confirmation

### Examples
- **Good entry example:** Strong company, technical breakout
- **Bad timing example:** Strong company, bought at resistance
- **False breakout:** Technical signal without fundamental backing

### Visual Ideas
- Annotated stock chart with support/resistance
- Moving average crossover
- Volume spike examples

---

## Pillar 3: News-Flow

### Key Concepts to Cover
- Earnings surprises (positive/negative)
- Sector rotation and market context
- Catalyst events (product launches, regulatory changes)
- Sentiment shifts

### Examples
- **Positive catalyst:** Earnings beat drives stock higher
- **Negative catalyst:** Regulatory news tanks stock
- **Market context:** Good company falls due to sector weakness

### Visual Ideas
- News headline overlays on charts
- Earnings surprise impact (before/after)
- Sector performance comparison
