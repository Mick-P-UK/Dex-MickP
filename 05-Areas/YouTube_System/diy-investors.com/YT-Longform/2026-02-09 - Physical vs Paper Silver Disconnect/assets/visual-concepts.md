## ACCESSIBILITY & LOCALIZATION NOTES

### Closed Captions:
- Full script transcription required for YouTube auto-sync
- Review auto-generated captions for accuracy (financial terminology)
- Consider professional caption file (.srt) for precision

### Visual Accessibility:
- High contrast text (white on dark, or dark on light)
- Large, legible fonts (minimum 24pt for on-screen text)
- Color-blind friendly palette (avoid red/green only distinctions)
- Clear annotations with sufficient duration for reading

### Localization Potential:
- Graphics designed with minimal text (easy to translate)
- Separate text layers in After Effects for future language versions
- Universal visual metaphors (charts, icons) translate well
