## Platform-Specific Guidelines

### YouTube Shorts
- **Duration:** 15-60 seconds (optimal: 30-45 seconds)
- **Aspect ratio:** Vertical 9:16
- **Hook:** First 3 seconds critical
- **CTA:** "Watch the full video" + link in description
- **Text overlays:** Yes - many watch without sound
- **Hashtags:** 3-5 relevant hashtags in description

### X/Twitter
- **Duration:** 30-90 seconds (optimal: 45-60 seconds)
- **Aspect ratio:** Square 1:1 or vertical 9:16
- **Hook:** First 2 seconds critical (auto-plays)
- **CTA:** "Full breakdown in thread" or "Link in bio"
- **Text overlays:** Yes - auto-plays without sound
- **Caption:** Tweet that complements video, not repeats it

### LinkedIn
- **Duration:** 30-120 seconds (optimal: 60-90 seconds)
- **Aspect ratio:** Square 1:1 or horizontal 16:9
- **Hook:** Professional but attention-grabbing
- **CTA:** "Link to full video in comments" or "Follow for more"
- **Text overlays:** Minimal - professional aesthetic
- **Caption:** LinkedIn post that adds value, tags relevant people

### Instagram Reels
- **Duration:** 15-60 seconds (optimal: 30-45 seconds)
- **Aspect ratio:** Vertical 9:16
- **Hook:** Visual + text overlay
- **CTA:** "Full video in bio" or "Save this for later"
- **Text overlays:** Yes - trendy styling
- **Hashtags:** 5-10 hashtags, mix popular and niche
