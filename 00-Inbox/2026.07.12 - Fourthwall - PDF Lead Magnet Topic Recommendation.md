## Summary

The strongest single PDF lead magnet for the Fourthwall Bronze funnel is a hybrid: an interactive Three Pillars scoring worksheet titled "The DIY Investor's Stock Score Sheet - A Three Pillars Buy Test". It combines the highest-quality lead format from the research (interactive Consideration-tier asset, endowment effect) with Mick's proprietary brand framework (Three Pillars of DIY-Investing), and demonstrates the exact methodology that Silver Inner Circle applies systematically each year.

Two runners-up are recommended as parallel or sequenced assets. Two of the notebook's five candidates should NOT be built as the primary asset.

Source notebook: PDF Lead Magnet - DIY Investors_Updated:2026.07.12 (NotebookLM). 131 sources: 1 synthesised research report + 130 web sources + seed brief.

## Key Takeaways

- Winning topic + format: interactive Three Pillars scoring worksheet ("The DIY Investor's Stock Score Sheet"). Consideration-tier asset, endowment effect, uses Mick's own brand IP, demonstrates Silver methodology
- Research is unambiguous: Consideration-tier assets (calculators, benchmark reports, specific-audience guides) produce HIGH-quality leads. Awareness-tier assets (quizzes, generic checklists, static ebooks) produce HIGH volume but LOW quality
- Interactive inputs (endowment effect) convert 2x to 3x higher than static PDFs
- The 50-page ebook is a proven anti-pattern - "speed to value" beats depth for lead magnets
- Multi-field forms kill CVR (1 field = 13.4%, 8 fields = 2.4%). Email only.
- Nurture sequence matters as much as the asset itself: Day 0 deliver -> Day 2 case study -> Day 4 objection-handler -> Day 7 social proof -> Day 10 direct CTA. Advisors using this see 20-30% download-to-call conversion vs 2% for "send and hope"

## The Winning Recommendation

### The DIY Investor's Stock Score Sheet - A Three Pillars Buy Test

Format: Interactive scoring worksheet PDF (fillable form fields OR printable + pencil-in)

Core promise (14 words): Rate any share against Mick's Three Pillars in 15 minutes before you buy it.

How it works:
- Reader enters a ticker they are considering
- Three scoring sections mirroring Mick's Three Pillars framework:
  - Fundamental Analysis - 4 to 5 yes/no or 1 to 5 scored questions (e.g. profitable last 3 years, positive free cash flow, reasonable P/E vs sector, insider buying, debt manageable)
  - Technical Analysis - 4 to 5 scored questions (e.g. above 200-day moving average, higher highs and higher lows, RSI not overbought, volume confirming price, sector rotation favourable)
  - News-Flow - 4 to 5 scored questions (e.g. recent RNS announcements read, no upcoming earnings shock risk, no pending regulatory issue, competitor position understood, macro tailwind or headwind identified)
- Simple scoring rubric (Go / Wait / Avoid) based on totals
- One worked example of a real share (past-tense trade) filled in end-to-end
- Sign-off page: "This is how I filter every stock I consider. The Silver Inner Circle membership is where I run this same filter systematically across the entire UK market every year for you."

Why it wins the ICP pressure-test:

| ICP filter | How this asset satisfies it |
|------------|------------------------------|
| Primary pain: "picking winning stocks and beating the market" | Direct: gives a repeatable filter for stock selection |
| "I wish I'd bought earlier" / "I missed that one" | Filter is fast enough to run before hesitation kills the trade |
| "Got my fingers burnt" | Systematic filter prevents emotional buys |
| "Make money, not lose money" | Rules-based system removes emotion |
| "Too much emotion, not enough analysis" | The tool IS analysis-first structure |
| Fears: wrong risk/reward for life stage | Fundamental section flags overvalued or fragile picks |
| Aspiration: empowerment to DYOR | Reader does the analysis themselves, framework provided |
| Objection: self-doubt | Structured worksheet gives confidence in the process |
| Brand: Three Pillars framework | This IS the Three Pillars, in worksheet form |
| Brand: transparent about wins AND losses | Worked example can show a loss and what the filter would have flagged |
| Brand: DYOR philosophy | Reader does own research, tool just structures it |
| Brand: no "certainty" or "guaranteed" language | Score is a decision aid, not a prediction |

Why it wins on the research pressure-test:

- Interactive fillable format triggers the endowment effect (2x to 3x conversion uplift)
- Consideration-tier asset -> high quality leads, not freebie seekers
- Actionable in 15 minutes -> avoids the 50-page-homework trap
- Rules-based system -> illusion-of-control lever (procedural agency)
- Authority signal -> Mick's proprietary framework (not generic)
- Clear next step -> Silver membership is the systematic version of the same tool

Channel to Silver Inner Circle:
The Silver tier includes "annual stock filtering" - Mick already does this at scale. The PDF proves the methodology on ONE stock; Silver runs it every year across the whole UK market. The upgrade is not "buy something new", it is "let me do the tedious version for you". This is the strongest natural upsell path in the whole business profile.

Development effort: LOW. A fillable PDF (via any modern PDF tool) plus a worked example is a 1-2 day build. No developer required.

## Runners-Up

### Runner-up 1: The DIY Investor's Portfolio Health Check

Format: Self-assessment worksheet PDF

Core promise: Audit your current portfolio's risk exposure in 20 minutes against seven objective criteria.

Why it is a runner-up not the winner:
- Same interactive/endowment strengths as the winner
- BUT requires an EXISTING portfolio to score - excludes cold traffic without holdings
- Winner scores ONE stock (accessible to everyone), Health Check scores a portfolio (existing-investor only)

When to build it:
- As a SECOND lead magnet for the segment of Mick's audience who already have portfolios but need direction (potentially the highest-value Silver conversion segment)
- Or as a Day-14 nurture asset in the sequence, after the primary lead magnet lands

Channel to Silver: strongest of the five - reveals gaps that Silver "portfolio guidance" directly plugs.

### Runner-up 2: Autopsy of a Trade - Why We Lose and How to Protect Your Capital

Format: Case study PDF (5 real trades, all losses, with the rules that would have flagged each)

Core promise: A transparent breakdown of five real trading losses and the exact rules that would have prevented them.

Why it is a runner-up not the winner:
- Emotionally powerful and unique in the finance-marketing landscape (few competitors publish their losses)
- Directly matches "Got my fingers burnt" verbatim ICP language
- BUT: case study format has lower CVR than interactive assets; harder to see immediate personal application
- BUT: the Silver channel is less direct - trust builds trust, but doesn't obviously funnel

When to build it:
- As the Day-4 objection-handler asset in the primary lead magnet's nurture sequence (research nurture framework specifically calls for a Day-4 objection asset)
- Or as a standalone secondary lead magnet targeting the "burnt investor coming back" segment

Brand fit is exceptional - transparency about losses is a stated brand differentiator that most competitors will not touch.

## Format Design Principles (from research)

Apply these to whichever asset gets built:

1. Speed to value: reader must get a "quick win" in under 20 minutes. No 50-page ebooks.
2. Interactive over static: fillable fields, scoring, personal inputs = endowment effect
3. Email-only opt-in: never more than one form field. 1 field = 13.4% CVR; 8+ = 2.4%
4. Mobile-first PDF: tagged for accessibility, dark-mode legible, single-column layout, no pinch-and-zoom needed
5. Clear next step at the end: the PDF's back page IS the Silver invitation
6. Compliance-clean UK framing: FCA "Fair, Clear, Not Misleading" plus Consumer Duty. No "certainty" or "guaranteed" words. Standalone compliant even on a single share.
7. Authority signal in the first 30 seconds: title, cover, first paragraph must position Mick's operator-level knowledge (retired Chartered Quantity Surveyor, full-time investor, live real-money portfolios)
8. Loss aversion framing beats gain framing: "avoid the mistake" outperforms "capture the upside" in this niche

## Nurture Sequence to Silver Inner Circle

Research finding: advisors using a structured 10-day sequence convert 20 to 30 percent of downloads to booked calls or paid signups, versus 2 percent for "send and hope".

Recommended sequence for whichever primary lead magnet is chosen:

| Day | Asset | Purpose |
|-----|-------|---------|
| 0 | Deliver PDF, welcome email, Silver invitation on back page | Immediate value + Silver seed |
| 2 | Case study email: "Here is how one Silver member used the Three Pillars filter to catch a 40 percent gainer" | Social proof plus methodology demo |
| 4 | Objection-handler email: "But I have been burnt before - here are five losses I have taken and what I learned" (this is the "Autopsy of a Trade" content, delivered as email) | Address "Got my fingers burnt" objection |
| 7 | Social proof: testimonials from existing Silver members, Plaza portfolio performance ("more than tripled since Jan 2020") | Third-party credibility |
| 10 | Direct CTA: Silver Inner Circle invitation with a specific reason to join THIS month (upcoming webinar, current annual filter, whatever is topical) | Conversion event |
