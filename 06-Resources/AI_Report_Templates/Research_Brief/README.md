## Placeholder Fields

| Placeholder | Replace with |
|-------------|-------------|
| `[COMPANY NAME]` | Full company name, dark blue 26pt bold |
| `[TICKER] | [EXCHANGE] | [SECTOR]` | e.g. SAVE.L | London Stock Exchange | African Oil, Gas & Renewables |
| `[REPORT SUBTITLE/EVENT] | [DATE]` | e.g. Stock Research Brief -- Plaza Group Webinar, 18 February 2026 |
| `[SECTION HEADING]` | First section heading |
| `[Report content goes here]` | Body content |
