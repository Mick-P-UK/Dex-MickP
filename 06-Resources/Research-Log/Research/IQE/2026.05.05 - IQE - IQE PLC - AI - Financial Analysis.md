## Detailed Analysis

GBP118.0m in FY 2024 due to global macroeconomic uncertainty and customer

driven by US military/defence programmes, AI, data centre demand, and new 
handset introductions brought FY 2025 revenue guidance to GBP97m [12, 13]. For 
2026, management forecasts >20% YoY revenue growth, backed by a strong Q1 order
book [14, 15].

margins [16].

2024 [2]. However, adjusted EBITDA for FY 2025 is guided to be "at least 
GBP2.0m", proving that IQE's cost-restructuring is yielding a return to 
operational profitability [16].
