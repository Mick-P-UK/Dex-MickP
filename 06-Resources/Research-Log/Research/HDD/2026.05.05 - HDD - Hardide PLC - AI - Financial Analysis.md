## Introduction
Hardide PLC (HDD) is a developer and provider of advanced surface coating 
technology [1]. The company specialises in patented tungsten carbide and 
tungsten metal matrix coatings applied via a unique "low" temperature (under 
500oC) Chemical Vapour Deposition (CVD) process [2]. These coatings 
significantly improve the life of components operating in aggressive 
environments by providing resistance to abrasion, erosion, and corrosion [3]. 
The company operates production facilities in Bicester, UK, and Martinsville, 
Virginia, US [2].
