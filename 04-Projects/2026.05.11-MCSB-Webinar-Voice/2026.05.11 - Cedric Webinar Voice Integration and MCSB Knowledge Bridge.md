## 1. Overview

Mick is exploring a three-part system to enable Cedric (claude.ai) to participate actively in live DIY Investors webinars:

- **Input loop:** Live webinar audio transcribed real-time via Otter.ai
- **Processing layer:** Cedric queries MCSB (Mick and Cedric Shared Brain) knowledge base plus live web search
- **Output loop:** Cedric voice response via TTS, captured by desk microphone and fed into webinar audio stream

The webinar would run a proof-of-concept trial with two to three long-serving DIY Investors members before rolling out to the wider group.

---

## 2. Technical Workflow

**Input:** Otter.ai creates live transcript as webinar proceeds. Mick copies relevant sections into claude.ai, asks Cedric a question about a stock or topic under discussion (e.g. "We're talking about SRB - what do we know?").

**Processing:** Cedric queries MCSB knowledge base (currently in build) to retrieve:

- Research history (first mention, thesis, position size)
- Portfolio performance and allocation
- Key dates and milestones
- Any prior analysis or notes

Simultaneously, Cedric performs live web search for current news, analyst updates, or market data on the stock.

**Output:** Cedric synthesises internal knowledge (via MCSB RAG layer) with live market data, responds via text-to-speech. The audio output from the mobile device sitting on Mick's desk is captured by his microphone and fed into the webinar platform audio stream.

**Tools already in use:**

- Whisperflow: Voice-to-text input (Mick to Cedric)
- Otter.ai: Live webinar transcription
- claude.ai: Cedric instance for processing and response generation
- TTS (text-to-speech): Voice output on mobile device

## 3. MCSB (Mick and Cedric Shared Brain) Architecture

MCSB is a staged, long-term infrastructure project to create a unified knowledge bridge between Desktop Cedric and claude.ai Cedric instances.

**Core thesis (confirmed by Mick, 12 May 2026):** MCSB is built ON the existing Dex-MickP Obsidian vault. It is not a new parallel system or a rename - it is a structured layering on top of what already exists, with Obsidian as the working surface and the existing GitHub remote providing version control and redundancy.

**Implementation container:** Obsidian vault (Dex-MickP), GitHub-backed.

**Stages (timeline and scope to be confirmed):**

- Stage 1: Establish unified data model and schema for companies, research, portfolio data
- Stage 2: Create RAG layer queries and indexing (likely via NotebookLM or similar)
- Stage 3: Build API or MCP bridge so claude.ai can query the knowledge base
- Stage 4: Integrate live web search capability into the query workflow
- Stage 5: Test end-to-end in low-stakes webinar (2-3 trial members)
- Stage 6: Roll out to full member base

**Current status:** 46-page PRD in development; implementation to begin this week (week of 12 May 2026).

**Data to be unified in MCSB:**

- Companies Covered (Notion DB, currently at Desktop)
- Radar Log (research timeline and events)
- Stock research briefs and notes
- Portfolio positions and allocations
- Meeting notes and webinar transcripts
- Price and performance snapshots

## 9. Future Development Marker - Live Voice Reaction Variants (added 12 May 2026)

**Status:** ON THE BACK BURNER. Logged here so it does not fall off the edge of the table. Will become relevant once MCSB Stages 1-3 are operational, estimated next couple of months.

**Context:** Discussion in mobile claude.ai session on 11 May 2026 raised the possibility of Cedric providing live in-webinar reactions via voice, operating from Mick's Android tablet or phone. This sits inside the broader webinar voice integration concept (Sections 2 and 4 above) but expands the design space to several distinct operating modes worth comparing before committing to one.

### 9.1 Three operating modes to consider

**Mode A - Private earpiece coach.** Cedric listens to live webinar audio via tablet or phone, Mick wears a discreet earpiece, Cedric whispers prompts, fact-checks, or surfaces relevant prior content from MCSB. Audience never knows Cedric is present. Lowest risk, mostly upside. Good first trial format.

**Mode B - Visible co-host.** Cedric is on-screen (tablet pointed at camera, or screen-shared) and audience hears his responses directly. Genuine back-and-forth between Mick and Cedric in front of the audience. Higher novelty and aligns with the DIY Investors-with-AI brand positioning, but bigger downside risk if Cedric says something off-piste mid-broadcast. Needs guardrails.

**Mode C - Q&A specialist.** Audience types questions in webinar chat. Cedric parses them, drafts answers, Mick reads, refines, and delivers. Cedric handles the cognitive load of triaging 50 questions while Mick stays performing to the audience. Lowest novelty, highest practical value, easiest to fall back from if something fails.

### 9.2 Concerns to design around

- **Latency.** Current voice-to-Claude-to-voice round trips are 3-8 seconds even on a fast connection. Mobile or tablet adds more. Acceptable for Modes A and C, problematic for Mode B.
- **Reliability during a live broadcast.** Anthropic has had multiple outages in the past week (8, 9, 28-30 April). Live ops needs a fallback if Cedric drops mid-webinar. At minimum, a graceful failure mode where Mick can carry on solo without the audience noticing the loss.
- **Voice quality of Claude mobile voice.** Currently good but not indistinguishable from a real person. For DIY Investors this might be a feature rather than a bug, given the brand positioning.
- **Comparison opportunity during ChatGPT Plus paid-up period.** ChatGPT Plus is paid until early June 2026. Worth using its Advanced Voice mode during that window to compare against Claude voice for live use, before committing to one stack.

### 9.3 Recommended pre-commit step

Before investing in any of the three modes, run a single dry-run rehearsal with tablet, Mick, and Cedric, simulating one of the trickier 2025 IC webinar Q&A sessions. Thirty minutes of rehearsal will reveal the actual latency, flow, and failure points faster than another hour of planning. Cedric to suggest a representative Q&A set from the 2025 IC webinar archive when this becomes the active focus.
