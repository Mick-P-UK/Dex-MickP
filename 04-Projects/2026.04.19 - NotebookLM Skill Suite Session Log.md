## Conventions Updated During Session

INDEX TITLE FORMAT (updated during Test 3):
Old: Index_Updated:YYYY.MM.DD
New: Index_Updated:YYYY.MM.DD - HH.MM
Reason: Prevents ambiguity when old and new index copies coexist.
        HH.MM uses dots (consistent with date format). Always delete
        the earlier-timestamped copy; keep the later one.
