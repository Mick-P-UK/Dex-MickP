# Fourthwall Digital Product Transaction Fees
Notebook:   Fourthwall_Updated:2026.07.12

Query:      "What flat transaction fee does Fourthwall charge for digital products, and are there any additional payment processing fees on top of that?"
============================================================

## Answer

Fourthwall charges a 5% flat transaction fee on digital product sales. This
fee drops to 0% if you upgrade to the Fourthwall Pro plan.

On top of the platform fee, standard payment processing fees also apply and
vary by the customer's chosen payment method. US-based credit card transactions
carry a typical 2.9% + $0.30 processing fee; PayPal applies its own rates
(around 3.49% + a fixed per-transaction fee). So the effective total cost of a
sale is (5% or 0% Pro) + the payment gateway's own percentage.
